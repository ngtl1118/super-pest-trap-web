Interactive FAQ Knowledge Matrix
Q: How do I know when to change the trap cartridges or scent sachets?

A: You don't need to perform any manual trap checks. The system features full automation and will automatically send a push alert to the mobile app only when the biodegradable bamboo fiber cartridge reaches full capacity and needs disposal. You simply replace the cartridge and natural scent sachet (coffee/pandan) at that moment.
Q: What daily or long-term maintenance does the electronic Smart Dock require?

A: It requires zero daily manual interaction. The dock operates on a rechargeable lithium battery and uses miniature thermal cameras alongside weight/infrared sensors to monitor activity. Maintenance is limited to charging the battery when prompted and ensuring the sensor lenses stay clean to prevent false positives.
Q: Do I need to manually clear or manage the data for health inspectors?

A: No. The system continuously archives analytics and tracking history automatically on a cloud computing platform. When health authorities conduct an inspection, restaurant managers can instantly pull up the recorded data via the mobile app to prove compliance.
